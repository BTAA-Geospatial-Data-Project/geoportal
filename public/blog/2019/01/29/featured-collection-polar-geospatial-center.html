Featured Collection: Polar Geospatial Center

Jan 29, 2019

Polar Geospatial Center

What is in this collection?

398 digitized maps that reflect multiple areas from both poles.

What geographic region is featured?

Both poles – Antarctica and Greenland being most common.

What BTAA library submitted the collection?

University of Minnesota

Interesting tidbits:

  • 9 of the maps are sketch maps.
  • Some maps were created by the U.S. War Department during World War II, for example Amagak, Greenland.
  • One of the items is a 1956 map that covers Antarctic exploration and different nations’ claims.

Antarctica Explorations and Claims, 1956

Where can I find out more?

Visit the website for the Polar Geospatial Center hosted at the University of Minnesota.
